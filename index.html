How we can help

Brain Snarl allows you to track the various aspects of your learning life.

classes - keep track of the homework and due dates from online and offline classes you are taking. Import homework from Coursera , Udacity and edX .

books - keep track of the books you need to master your skils. Import/export to goodreads .

meetups - find and keep track of meetups. Search and import from Meetup .

FAQ

Why don't you import/export X online service?
There are a lot of online services and formats. We're working to support as many as we can, but it could take some time. Feel free to drop us a line (info at brainsnarl.com) and let us know which service is most valuable to you.
Why don't I just use a todo list?
You could, but then you wouldn't get all the extra features we will be rolling out. We're planning on tightly integrating Brain Snarl with other online services and adding helpful analytics to unleash your mind!
